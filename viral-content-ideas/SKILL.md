# Viral Instagram Content Ideas Generator

Generates high-impact Instagram content ideas for any niche, business, or website — optimized for virality, saves, and reach.

---

## Step 1: Collect Business & Niche Details

Before generating ideas, ask the user for the following (if not already provided):

| Field | Example |
|---|---|
| **Nicho principal** | Google Ads, SEO, Marketing Digital, E-commerce, etc. |
| **Nome do negócio/marca** | Agência X, Consultoria Y, Curso Z |
| **Website ou produto** | URL ou descrição do serviço |
| **Público-alvo** | Empreendedores, freelancers, donos de e-commerce, etc. |
| **Objetivo no Instagram** | Crescimento, leads, autoridade, vendas |
| **Quantidade de ideias** | Padrão: 30 ideias (1 mês de conteúdo) |
| **Idioma dos posts** | Padrão: Português (BR) |

If the user says "gera ideias para meu nicho de X" without details, ask before generating. Don't assume defaults.

## Step 3: Generate the Content Ideas Table

Generate 30 content ideas organized in a structured table:

| # | Formato | Gancho (Hook) | Tema Central | Objetivo | Viral Score |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

### Formato Options:
- **Carrossel** — multi-slide educational post (best for saves/shares)
- **Reels** — short video (best for reach)
- **Foto + Legenda** — single image with strong caption
- **Stories** — behind the scenes, polls, Q&A
- **Meme/Humor** — relatable content for shares

### Viral Score (1–5 stars):
Rate each idea based on expected viral potential (5 = extremely viral, 1 = brand awareness).

---

## Step 4: Top 5 Priority Posts (Deep Dive)

After the table, highlight the 5 highest-scoring ideas and for each provide:

- Formato, Objetivo, Viral Score
- GANCHO (hook — first line of caption or slide 1)
- ESTRUTURA DO CONTEÚDO (slide-by-slide for carousel, script for reels, or full caption for photo)
- HASHTAGS SUGERIDAS (5–10 relevant hashtags)
- CTA (clear call to action)

## Virality Rules for Instagram (2025)

1. **Salvas > Curtidas** — Content that gets saved is amplified more by the algorithm
2. **Primeiros 3 segundos** — The hook must stop the scroll in under 3 seconds
3. **Slides 1 e 2** — On carousels, slide 1 must promise value; slide 2 must deliver the first insight
4. **Compartilhamentos** — Content that makes people think "preciso mandar isso para alguém" wins
5. **Comentários de qualidade** — Questions and debates in comments boost reach significantly
6. **Consistência de nicho** — Stay focused on one niche; the algorithm learns who to show your content to
7. **Áudio em Reels** — Use trending audio when possible for extra distribution

---

## Output Format Rules

- Always respond in Português (BR) unless the user specifies otherwise
- Use emojis strategically in hooks and CTAs (not excessively)
- Keep hooks under 10 words — shorter is stronger
- Every idea must have a clear, measurable objective (saves, reach, leads, etc.)
- After delivering all 30 ideas, ask: "Quais posts você quer desenvolver em detalhes? Posso criar o carrossel completo ou o roteiro do Reels para qualquer um deles."
